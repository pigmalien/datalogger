1. Click the **paint palette icon** (ğŸ¨) in the top-right corner of the navigation bar
2. Scroll through the theme list
3. Click on any theme name to apply it instantly
4. Your choice is automatically saved to your browser

## Theme Recommendations

### For Health & Medical Tracking
- **Corporate** - Professional and easy to read
- **Light** - Clean and clinical
- **Business** - Serious and focused
- **Nord** - Calm and soothing

### For Daily Use
- **Emerald** - Fresh and energizing
- **Garden** - Calming and natural
- **Pastel** - Gentle on the eyes
- **Cupcake** - Cheerful and friendly

### For Night Logging
- **Dark** - Classic dark mode
- **Dim** - Softer, less harsh

- **Dracula** - Popular among developers

### For Fun
- **Synthwave** - Retro-futuristic vibes

## Tips

- **Try different themes** throughout the day - light themes for daytime, dark for evening
- **Match your mood** - themes can affect how you feel about tracking
- **Consider readability** - some themes have better contrast for charts
- **Share your favorite** - recommend themes to others using the app

## Accessibility

All DaisyUI themes are designed with accessibility in mind, but some offer better contrast than others:

**High Contrast Themes:**
- Light
- Dark
- Black
- Corporate
- Business

**Lower Contrast (Aesthetic) Themes:**
- Pastel
- Cupcake
- Lo-Fi
- Dim

Choose based on your visual needs and preferences!
